Hello, world. I think about these words a lot.

This is my website. It's not much, but it's mine. I like it and it works.

I work on design systems and developer experience for front-end teams. A lot of that work comes down to clarity. I want to find the right abstraction, name things well, and explain why one approach works better than another in a given context.

Writing feels like a natural extension of that. A way to work through ideas, share what I'm learning, and maybe help someone else wrestling with similar problems.

So here we are. I rebuilt this site. I moved from Gatsby to Next.js and added a simple blog. Plain markdown files, nothing fancy. We'll see where it goes.
