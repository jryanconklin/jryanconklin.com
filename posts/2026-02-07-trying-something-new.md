I've been thinking about writing more. Not in a "I should have a blog" way, but more like, "maybe putting thoughts into words will help me think better."

I spend a lot of time working on design systems and helping teams figure out how to build things. A lot of that work is about clarity. Finding the right abstraction, naming things well, explaining why one approach might work better than another.

Writing feels like a natural extension of that. A way to work through ideas, share what I'm learning, and maybe help someone else who's wrestling with similar problems.

So here we are. I rebuilt this site (moved from Gatsby to Next.js, finally) and added a simple blog setup. Plain markdown files, nothing fancy. We'll see if this becomes a regular thing or just sits here as a nice idea that never went anywhere.

Either way, it's here now. And that feels like a good start.
